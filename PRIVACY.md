# Privacy Policy for Tabulosa

**Last updated: March 26, 2026**

## Introduction

Tabulosa ("we", "our", or "the Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our browser extension handles information when you use it.

## Information We Collect

**We do not collect any personal information.**

Tabulosa operates entirely within your browser. All data is stored locally on your device using the browser's built-in storage API. This includes:

- Your language preferences
- Your CEFR/JLPT level selections
- Words you have learned
- Words you have saved

## How We Use Information

The locally stored data is used solely to:

- Remember your language and level preferences
- Track your learning progress
- Provide a personalized learning experience

## Data Sharing

**We do not sell, trade, or transfer your data to any third parties.**

All data remains on your local device and is never transmitted to external servers.

## Third-Party Services

When you click the dictionary link for a word, you will be redirected to an external dictionary website (such as SpanishDict, Jisho.org, etc.). These third-party websites have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

## Text-to-Speech

The extension uses your browser's built-in Web Speech API for pronunciation. This feature operates locally within your browser.

## Children's Privacy

Our Extension does not collect any personal information from users of any age, including children under 13.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date above.
